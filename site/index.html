Frequently Asked Questions

Do you remove valuable wood in exchange for the lumber?
Unfortunately no. The high cost of vehicles, machinery, labor, insurance, and fuel to cut and process the wood versus the value of the wood itself doesn’t enable us to do that.
Do you carry common building-supply wood like 2x4’s?
We cut larger wood specific for custom needs. Common building-supply wood can and should be purchased cheaper through stores like Home Depot and Lowes due to their mass production of it.
Can you turn MY tree into lumber I keep?
Yes — that’s our specialty. We can take down your tree and mill it on site with our portable band sawmill, or bring it back to our stationary mill in Nassau. You end up with slabs, beams, or a finished piece from a tree you already own. Call us and tell us about the tree.
