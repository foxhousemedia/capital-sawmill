Firewood Prices

Seasoned mixed hardwoods, cut to stove lengths of 14″ to 16″ and stored under a pavilion so your wood stays clean and dry. Sold in face cord increments, priced for local delivery:

  • $230 — face cord (4x8, one tier), dumped
  • $385 — double face cord (4x8, two tiers), dumped
  • $540 — triple face cord (4x8, three tiers), dumped

Kindling kegs — 11″ diameter, 14″ long, with a convenient carrying handle — are $15 and make starting a fire a snap.

Smoker Wood & Camp Wood

Smoker chunks in pear, apple, hickory and cherry for $14.95 a bag (about 14″ x 14″), and smoker shavings in cherry and cherry/maple for $4.95 (14″ x 20″). Camp wood available too — call ahead and we’ll have it ready.
